## Additional Qualities used here

A few that aren't on the base table:

- **Cavalry.** When attacking from a mount, gain +1 [CD] damage.
- **Cumbersome.** May not be used in tight quarters (corridors, indoors, on most ships); difficulty +1 in such conditions.
- **Naval.** Reduces the penalty for unstable footing (decks, rigging) by 1.
- **Slow Draw.** Drawing the weapon is a Standard Action, not a Minor.
- **Ignores Shield.** A shield's bonus to Parry difficulty doesn't apply against this weapon.
- **No Damage on miss.** A miss with this weapon inflicts none of the secondary status (no entanglement, no fall).
- **Disarming.** When this weapon scores an Effect, you may immediately attempt a Disarm at 1 Momentum less than normal.

## Quick reference

- **Damage** in Combat Dice [CD]. Read 1→1, 2→2, 3–4→0, 5–6→1 + Effect.
- **Vicious X** is the single most lethal property — each Effect rolled adds X damage.
- **Range bands**: Close · Medium · Long · Extreme. Outside listed range = +1 difficulty per step.
- **Reload Minor / Standard** — what action it costs to reload between shots.
- **Two-handed** weapons free up the off-hand for nothing else; **Unbalanced** trades follow-up defense for damage.
