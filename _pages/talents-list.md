This page sketches a usable set of talents for the 2d20 system as run in the Hyborian Age. Names are functional and mechanics-focused; each effect is one or two sentences, in my own words, fitted to the framework documented in [Talents]({{ '/talents/' | relative_url }}). Use them, swap them, rewrite them — they're a starting reference, not a fixed list.

Unless otherwise stated:

- Each talent assumes the parent skill is the one named in its section.
- "Once per scene" / "once per session" cooldowns reset at the named interval.
- Effects that improve a roll do not stack with another talent that grants the same kind of improvement on the same roll — take the larger benefit.
- Where a talent says "*Prereq: X*", you must already have talent X to take this one.

---

## Acrobatics

- **Tumbler.** Falls of up to one zone deal no damage. Longer falls have their damage reduced by 2 [CD].
- **Cat's Landing.** *Prereq: Tumbler.* You may convert any fall into a free move toward a target zone instead of taking damage, once per scene.
- **Reckless Vault.** When you spend 1 Momentum to Sprint, you may move two zones instead of one.
- **Wall Run.** Treat vertical surfaces as difficult terrain instead of impassable for one move per scene.
- **Threading the Needle.** When you Dodge as a Reaction, on a successful test you may also move to an adjacent zone for free.
- **Acrobat's Footing.** Ignore the difficulty step normally imposed by "Unstable" terrain (rigging, scree, narrow ledges).

## Alchemy

- **Toxicologist.** Reduce the difficulty of identifying or neutralizing a poison by 1.
- **Brewmaster.** Doses of poison or restorative you brew yourself cost half as much in materials.
- **Forbidden Lore.** *Prereq: Toxicologist.* You may attempt *Sorcery* tests using *Alchemy* as the skill, at +1 difficulty.
- **Distilled Edge.** Once per scene, declare a vial of your own brew: gain +1d20 on an *Alchemy* test or have an ally restore 2 [CD] of Vigor.
- **Black Lotus.** *Prereq: Brewmaster.* Poisons you apply gain +1 to their Toxic value.

## Animal Handling

- **Bonded Mount.** Choose a single animal as your bond. It gains +2 Vigor and +1 to one attribute of your choice while you ride or work with it.
- **Pack Master.** You may handle up to three trained animals at once with a single test.
- **Beast Speech.** Your *Animal Handling* tests against animals never face the "untrained handler" penalty, and the gamemaster permits short, simple intent-level communication.
- **Tamer.** *Prereq: Beast Speech.* You may attempt to *Animal Handling* a hostile beast as if it were a person open to *Persuade*, once per scene.
- **Steady Reins.** While mounted, the difficulty of *Coordination*-based tests for combat or maneuvering on horseback is reduced by 1.

## Athletics

- **Iron Limbs.** Treat your effective Brawn as 1 higher for tests of raw strength (lifting, breaking, holding back).
- **Mountaineer.** Reduce the difficulty of climbing tests by 1, including on adverse surfaces.
- **Swift Strider.** Your free Move on your turn may be Sprint distance (one extra zone) once per scene without spending Momentum.
- **Tireless.** *Prereq: Iron Limbs.* You succeed automatically on the first *Athletics* or *Resistance* test of any travel day.
- **Heavy Lifter.** *Prereq: Iron Limbs.* You ignore the Encumbering quality on one piece of carried gear of your choice.
- **Sprinter's Heart.** Once per scene, take a Run action and still take a Standard Action that same turn.

## Command

- **Inspiring Cry.** As a Standard Action, *Command* (D1): allies who hear you each restore 1 [CD] of Resolve.
- **Rally.** *Prereq: Inspiring Cry.* Spend 2 Momentum to remove 1 Trauma from a single ally who can hear you.
- **Iron Voice.** Tests to intimidate hostile but rational opponents use *Command* instead of *Persuade*.
- **Battlefield Presence.** While you remain conscious, all allies within Close gain +1 Soak against mental damage.
- **Captain's Order.** Once per scene, an ally may use your *Command* Expertise in place of their own *Discipline* for a single Resistance to fear.

## Counsel

- **Steady Word.** When you assist a *Discipline* or *Resistance* test for an ally, your assist die scores a success on a 1 or 2 (instead of 1 only).
- **Healer of Hearts.** During downtime, remove 1 Trauma from an ally with a *Counsel* (D1) test instead of *Discipline*.
- **Mentor.** *Prereq: Steady Word.* While present, you may grant an ally +1 Expertise (max 5) on a single skill for the scene.
- **Confessor's Ear.** Once per scene, ask one direct emotional question; the gamemaster answers honestly.

## Craft

- **Master Smith.** Reduce the time required to repair or modify weapons and armor by half. Weapons you craft yourself have a chance to gain +1 to a single Quality (gamemaster's call).
- **Bowyer.** *Craft* tests to make or repair bows, crossbows, or arrows reduce difficulty by 1. Self-made bows count as +1 [CD] damage.
- **Trapsmith.** You may build a one-use trap as a Standard Action and an *Athletics* + materials test; trap damage equals 3 [CD] + your *Craft* Expertise.
- **Field Repair.** Once per scene, repair a single broken or damaged piece of gear with a *Craft* (D2) test.
- **Specialist.** Pick a single craft (e.g. armorsmith). Reduce difficulty by 1 for all tests within that specialty.

## Discipline

- **Iron Will.** When you take mental damage, reduce it by 1 [CD] (minimum 0).
- **Fearless.** *Prereq: Iron Will.* You may reroll a single d20 on any *Discipline* test against fear or sorcerous influence.
- **Pain Eater.** Once per scene, ignore the "test difficulty +1" penalty from carrying 1 Wound or Trauma for a single test.
- **Inured Mind.** *Prereq: Iron Will.* Increase your Courage by 1.
- **Center of Calm.** While at 0 Wounds, you may spend 1 Resolve to clear any single Condition (*Dazed*, *Restrained*, etc.) on yourself.
- **Unbroken.** *Prereq: Pain Eater.* You ignore the *Dying* state for one full round when you would first enter it.

## Healing

- **Field Surgeon.** Reduce the difficulty of *Healing* tests by 1 when treating Wounds, not just Vigor.
- **Herbalist.** When at least one hour of preparation in suitable terrain is available, restore an additional 2 [CD] of Vigor to a treated patient.
- **Battle Medic.** *Prereq: Field Surgeon.* Make First Aid a Minor Action instead of a Standard Action once per scene.
- **Soothing Hand.** Your *Healing* tests may restore Resolve in addition to or instead of Vigor.
- **Sleeper's Watch.** During a full night's rest under your care, allies remove one additional Wound from any tests they make.

## Insight

- **Quick Read.** Reduce the difficulty of *Insight* tests to assess an NPC's surface mood by 1.
- **Heart's Ear.** Once per scene, ask the gamemaster one specific question about an NPC's current motivation; they answer truthfully.
- **Deception Sense.** *Prereq: Quick Read.* When an NPC lies to you, you may immediately make an *Insight* (D1) test to spot it, even without declaring you were watching.
- **Cold Read.** Once per scene, declare a single factual claim about an NPC; the gamemaster either confirms or gives you a useful nudge.

## Linguistics

- **Polyglot.** You speak two additional languages of your choice fluently.
- **Cipher Breaker.** *Linguistics* tests against codes and ciphers reduce difficulty by 1.
- **Trader's Tongue.** *Prereq: Polyglot.* In any settled region, you can make yourself understood for basic needs without a test.
- **Tongue of the Old World.** *Prereq: Cipher Breaker.* You may attempt to read or transcribe a dead language at +1 difficulty.

## Lore

- **Sage.** Once per scene, when you successfully recall a fact via *Lore*, the gamemaster gives you one additional related detail at no Momentum cost.
- **Loremaster.** *Prereq: Sage.* You may attempt *Lore* tests on unfamiliar subjects at +1 difficulty instead of being unable to.
- **Library.** When you have access to a notable collection of texts, reduce the difficulty of *Lore* tests by 1.
- **Demonologist.** Reduce the difficulty of *Lore* tests concerning otherworldly creatures and beings by 1.

## Melee

- **Riposte.** When you succeed on a Parry Reaction, you may immediately spend 2 Momentum to make a single melee attack against the attacker.
- **Cleaving Blow.** When you take down an opponent with a melee attack, you may immediately make a free attack against another opponent at Reach.
- **Strong Arm.** When attacking with *Brawn + Melee*, add +1 [CD] to damage.
- **Deadly Footwork.** When attacking with *Agility + Melee*, you may move within the zone before or after the attack as a free action.
- **Killing Strike.** *Prereq: Cleaving Blow.* Once per scene, declare a Killing Strike before rolling. The attack inflicts a Wound on first damage that gets past Soak, regardless of remaining Vigor.
- **Brutal Press.** When you reduce an enemy's Vigor to 0 but do not kill them, immediately push them one zone away.
- **Two-Weapon Grace.** When fighting with two melee weapons, gain +1d20 to attacks; you may not also Parry that round.
- **Disarming Blow.** When you spend 2 Momentum for a Disarm secondary, the target also takes 1 [CD] of damage if your hit otherwise dealt none.

## Observation

- **Eagle Eye.** Reduce the difficulty of *Observation* tests at Long or Extreme range by 1.
- **Trained Watcher.** You cannot be surprised in any scene where you have at least one round of warning.
- **Detail Memory.** Once per scene, ask the gamemaster about a small detail of a place you've visited before; the answer is given honestly.
- **Hunter's Eye.** *Prereq: Eagle Eye.* When you Aim at an Observed target, gain +1 [CD] on the resulting ranged attack.

## Parry

- **Defensive Stance.** As a Minor Action, declare Defensive Stance. Until your next turn, your Parry Reactions gain +1d20.
- **Counter.** *Prereq: Defensive Stance.* When you score 3 or more successes on a Parry Reaction, you may make a Reach attack against the attacker as a free action.
- **Shield Wall.** When wielding a shield, allies within Reach gain +1 Soak against ranged attacks.
- **Twin Defense.** *Prereq: Two-Weapon Grace (Melee).* You may Parry with both weapons in a single round without the second Reaction costing Momentum.
- **Whirling Blades.** Once per scene, your Parry applies against all melee attacks made against you that round.
- **Untouchable.** *Prereq: Whirling Blades.* You may take a third Reaction in a round by spending 2 Momentum (instead of being limited to two).

## Persuade

- **Honeyed Tongue.** Reduce the difficulty of *Persuade* tests to seduce, charm, or befriend by 1.
- **Liar's Smile.** Reduce the difficulty of *Persuade* tests to deceive by 1.
- **Reading the Room.** *Prereq: Honeyed Tongue.* Once per scene, ask the gamemaster what an NPC currently wants. The answer is honest but brief.
- **Bargainer.** Reduce the cost of any single transaction in Gold by 25% on a successful *Persuade* (D1) test.
- **Demagogue.** *Prereq: Liar's Smile.* Your *Persuade* tests addressing a crowd of three or more apply to the crowd as a single target.
- **Silver Tongue.** Once per session, spend 2 Momentum after a failed *Persuade* test to reroll all your dice.

## Ranged Weapons

- **Quick Draw.** Drawing or readying a ranged weapon is a free action instead of a Minor.
- **Marksman.** Reduce the range-band penalty of a ranged weapon by one step.
- **Volley.** *Prereq: Marksman.* On a successful ranged attack against a tight formation, you may spend 1 Momentum to add a single additional target at the same zone.
- **Trick Shot.** *Prereq: Marksman.* Once per scene, declare a called shot for free instead of spending 2 Momentum.
- **Steady Aim.** When you Aim as a Minor Action, gain +2d20 on the next ranged attack instead of +1d20.
- **Long Eye.** *Prereq: Steady Aim.* You may attempt ranged attacks at Extreme range with all but bows and crossbows; bows count Extreme as Long.

## Resistance

- **Cast-Iron Gut.** Reduce the difficulty of *Resistance* tests against poison or disease by 1.
- **Slow to Bleed.** When you take a Wound from a single physical hit, reduce the wound's modifier on your next test by 1 (minimum 0).
- **Born Tough.** Increase your Vigor by 2.
- **Field Recovery.** *Prereq: Slow to Bleed.* You recover all Vigor between scenes even without a Healing test, provided you had a moment to catch your breath.
- **Bull's Constitution.** *Prereq: Born Tough.* Increase your Vigor by an additional 2.
- **Unbreakable.** *Prereq: Bull's Constitution.* The first time per scene you would take a Wound, you may instead lose 4 Vigor.

## Sailing

- **Old Salt.** Reduce the difficulty of *Sailing* tests in adverse weather by 1.
- **Storm Captain.** *Prereq: Old Salt.* In any extended *Sailing* test, you generate one bonus success per check on a successful roll.
- **Navigator.** When at sea, you cannot become lost so long as the stars are visible at least once a night.
- **Quartermaster.** Reduce shipboard supply consumption by half on long voyages.

## Society

- **Courtier.** Reduce the difficulty of *Society* tests at noble courts by 1.
- **Hidden Names.** Once per scene, declare a useful acquaintance in a city you've visited; the gamemaster decides who they are.
- **Whispered Connections.** *Prereq: Courtier.* You always know how to locate the local fence, smuggler, or information broker.
- **Letters of Introduction.** Once per session, produce a written reference from a notable patron; the gamemaster decides what it gets you.

## Sorcery

- **Patron's Boon.** Once per scene, reduce the Resolve cost of a single spell by 1.
- **Black Whisper.** *Prereq: Patron's Boon.* You may cast a *petty enchantment* as a Minor Action instead of a Standard.
- **Bound Familiar.** Bind a small animal as a sorcerous familiar. It scouts, fetches, and grants you +1 Awareness in its presence.
- **Sacrificial Casting.** *Prereq: Black Whisper.* Take 1 Wound to gain +1d20 on a single sorcery test (declared before rolling).
- **Sustained Spell.** Maintain a spell with a duration without taking further Standard Actions, by paying 1 Resolve at the start of each round.
- **Sorcerous Stamina.** *Prereq: Sustained Spell.* Increase your Resolve by 3.
- **Pact-Marked.** Choose a patron tradition; sorcery tests within that tradition reduce difficulty by 1.
- **Reflective Sight.** Once per scene, perceive a single magical effect within Long range without rolling.

## Stealth

- **Shadow Walk.** Reduce the difficulty of *Stealth* tests in poor lighting by 1.
- **Silent Strike.** When you make an attack from an unobserved position, add +2 [CD] of damage.
- **Camouflage.** *Prereq: Shadow Walk.* In wilderness terrain, you cannot be detected at Long range or further without a specific search.
- **Vanish.** Once per scene, after an attack from concealment, make an immediate free *Stealth* (D1) test to slip back out of sight.
- **Patient Hunter.** *Prereq: Camouflage.* You generate +1 Momentum on the first successful *Stealth* test of a scene.

## Survival

- **Pathfinder.** Reduce the difficulty of overland navigation tests by 1; double overland travel speed in familiar terrain.
- **Forager.** Generate food and water for the whole party with a *Survival* (D1) test instead of a Toughness check.
- **Wilderness Eye.** Tests to track or read sign in the wilderness reduce difficulty by 1.
- **Snare Maker.** *Prereq: Forager.* Build a basic snare or pit trap with one hour and a *Survival* (D1) test.
- **Storm-Wise.** You always know what weather the next twelve hours will bring; reduce the *Resistance* difficulty against weather effects by 1.

## Thievery

- **Light Fingers.** Reduce the difficulty of *Thievery* tests to pick pockets by 1.
- **Picklock.** Reduce the difficulty of *Thievery* tests against locks by 1; use lockpicks as if they were two grades better.
- **Eyes for Treasure.** *Prereq: Light Fingers.* You can identify the most valuable item in a small hoard at a glance.
- **Trap Sense.** Once per scene, declare you are watching for traps; the gamemaster will warn you of the next mechanical or alchemical trap before you trigger it.
- **Second-Story.** *Prereq: Picklock.* Reduce the difficulty of *Athletics* tests to climb walls or buildings by 1.

## Warfare

- **Tactician.** Once per scene, an ally within earshot adds +1d20 to a single combat test on your direction.
- **Siegecraft.** Reduce the difficulty of *Warfare* tests against fortified positions by 1.
- **Captain.** *Prereq: Tactician.* While you are leading a unit of Minions, the unit's Vigor counts as 1 higher per Minion.
- **Reading the Field.** When you make a successful *Warfare* (D1) test at the start of an action scene, all allies who hear you may exchange one Reaction for a Minor Action that turn.
- **Veteran.** *Prereq: Captain.* You suffer no difficulty penalty from carrying a single Wound while in combat.

---

## Quick reference

- All talents are bought with XP between sessions: **200** without a prereq, **400** with one.
- A talent with a prereq requires you to take the prereq first.
- Same-source bonuses don't stack — take the larger.
- "Once per scene" resets on scene change; "once per session" resets at the start of the next play session.
