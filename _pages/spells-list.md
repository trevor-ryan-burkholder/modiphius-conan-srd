This is a usable spell reference for the 2d20 sorcery rules. Each entry is an original sketch — name, costs, and a one-sentence mechanical effect in my own words — designed to fit the framework described in [Sorcery]({{ '/sorcery/' | relative_url }}). Use it as a starting catalog; rewrite, rename, or rebuild as your game demands.

Every spell entry lists, in order: **Tradition · Difficulty · Cost · Range · Duration**, followed by a brief effect, and where useful a Momentum spend or backlash note.

Cost notation:

- **R** = Resolve cost paid by the caster.
- **D** = Doom added to the gamemaster's pool.
- **Time** in addition to the above for ritual spells.
- **Components** noted in parentheses where required.

## Petty Enchantments

Available to any sorcerer with *Sorcery* Expertise 1+. Difficulty D0 or D1. Cost 0–1 Resolve. The parlor magic of the trade.

- **Whispered Light.** Petty · D0 · 0R · Reach · 1 scene. A small flame, glow, or floating mote of light, no brighter than a candle.
- **Hand of the Distant.** Petty · D1 · 1R · Close · 1 round. Move, lift, or toy with a small object within range without touching it.
- **Soothe the Beast.** Petty · D1 · 1R · Reach · 1 scene. A non-hostile animal becomes calm in your presence; cannot compel hostile beasts.
- **Sour Tongue.** Petty · D1 · 1R · Close · 1 scene. Curdles food, milk, or wine to inedibility. Useful for poisoners and ill-tempered cooks.
- **Reading the Mark.** Petty · D1 · 1R · Touch · Instant. Sense whether a touched item carries an active magical effect; tells you nothing about what.
- **Curtain of Glamour.** Petty · D1 · 1R · Self · 1 scene. Your clothes and face appear unremarkable in a crowd; +1d20 on social *Stealth* tests.

## Tradition: The Esoteric

Academic sorcery. Lower direct cost than other traditions, slower at the highest tier, drawn from libraries, secret schools, and the long memory of mages.

- **Cipher of the Black Pages.** Esoteric · D1 · 1R · Touch · Instant. Read any written language as if you knew it for the length of one passage; complex ciphers raise the difficulty.
- **Mind's Eye.** Esoteric · D2 · 2R · Long · 1 scene. Project your senses to a point you can see; you remain helpless while perceiving.
- **Sending.** Esoteric · D2 · 2R · Same continent · Instant. Deliver a short message (10 words) to a named recipient you have met; no reply.
- **Wall of Words.** Esoteric · D2 · 2R · Close · 1 scene. Inscribe a ward across an entrance; named beings cannot cross without a *Discipline* (D2) test.
- **Lesser Curse.** Esoteric · D2 · 2R · Long · 1 day. Raise the difficulty of all tests of one chosen kind (e.g., melee, social) for a named target by 1. Target may resist with *Discipline* (D2).
- **Greater Curse.** Esoteric · D3 · 3R + 1D · Long · until dispelled. As Lesser Curse, but +2 difficulty and persistent. Removing it requires equal or greater sorcery.
- **Compel.** Esoteric · D3 · 3R · Close · until command resolved or 1 scene. Force one simple instruction on a willing or weak-willed target. Hostile targets resist with *Discipline* (D3). The compulsion ends if it would clearly require death or impossible action.
- **Open the Lock.** Esoteric · D2 · 2R · Touch · Instant. Open any non-magical lock, latch, bolt, or knot.
- **Astral Mirror.** Esoteric · D3 · 3R · Same city · 1 scene. Briefly speak with another willing sorcerer who knows you; both must concentrate.
- **Ritual of Days.** Esoteric · D3 · 4R + Time (1 hour) · Touch · 1 week. Permanently bind a chosen effect (light, ward, divination) to an object for the duration.

## Tradition: The Bargained

Spells acquired by pact. Effects are bigger; the price runs longer than the casting.

- **Mark of the Patron.** Bargained · D1 · 1R · Touch · until met. Bind a willing creature to perform a single task; failure to perform draws the patron's notice.
- **The Patron's Hand.** Bargained · D2 · 2R + 1D · Long · Instant. Strike a single target for 4 [CD] mental damage, Intense, Fearsome 1.
- **Wreath of Coils.** Bargained · D2 · 2R + 1D · Close · 1 round. Conjure a constricting form (serpents, smoke, brambles) that Grapples a target. Lasts until a successful *Athletics* (D2) test breaks free.
- **Pact of Steel.** Bargained · D2 · 2R + 1D · Touch · 1 scene. A willing recipient gains +2 [CD] damage on their weapon attacks; at scene's end, take 1 Trauma.
- **Bargained Tongue.** Bargained · D2 · 2R + 1D · Close · 1 scene. Compel a single creature to answer one question truthfully. Refusing answers requires *Discipline* (D3).
- **Summon Lesser Servitor.** Bargained · D3 · 3R + 2D + Components (offering) · Close · 1 scene. Call a minor creature from elsewhere to perform one task. If untreated, it may turn upon the caller after the task is done.
- **Summon Greater Servitor.** Bargained · D4 · 4R + 3D + Components + Time (1 hour) · Close · 1 scene. As above, but a Toughened or Horror-class servitor. The patron names the price.
- **Black Sword.** Bargained · D3 · 3R + 1D + Components (blood) · Touch · 1 scene. A weapon becomes Vicious +1, Intense, and Fearsome 1 for the duration; bearer takes 1 Wound at scene's end.
- **Sign of the Pact.** Bargained · D3 · 4R + 2D · Long · 1 day. Mark a target. The caster knows their general location for the duration; the target dreams unwell.
- **The Patron's Demand.** Bargained · D4 · 5R + 3D + Time (1 night) · Touch · permanent. Bind a creature into long service; the gamemaster details the patron's reciprocal demand on the caster.

## Tradition: The Inherited

Spells of blood. Always-on or near-on, hard to refuse, and a kind of inheritance more than a study.

- **Black Eye.** Inherited · D1 · 1R · Close · 1 scene. A creature you stare at openly takes 1 Trauma and steps back as if struck. Once per target per scene.
- **Marked at Birth.** Inherited · D0 · — · Self · permanent. A small physical sign (eyes that catch fire wrong, paler skin) reveals you as carrying the line. +1d20 on *Persuade* tests with practitioners; –1d20 with mundane folk who recognize the sign.
- **The Old Hunger.** Inherited · D2 · 2R + 1D · Reach · 1 round. Drain 1 Vigor from a touched, unwilling creature; restore 1 Vigor to yourself.
- **Bone Listening.** Inherited · D2 · 2R · Touch · Instant. Hold the bones of a dead creature; learn one significant fact of how they died.
- **Speak with the Newly Dead.** Inherited · D2 · 2R + 1D · Touch · 5 questions. Ask a recently-killed creature up to five short questions; their answers are honest but bound by what they knew in life.
- **The Look That Withers.** Inherited · D3 · 3R + 2D · Long · 1 scene. A single target's *Brawn* drops by 2 for the duration. *Discipline* (D2) negates.
- **Walking Unseen.** Inherited · D3 · 3R · Self · 1 scene. You cannot be perceived by ordinary senses; broken if you take a hostile action.
- **Blood Recall.** Inherited · D2 · 2R + 1D · Touch · 1 round per success. Witness, through bond of blood, a moment of a relative's life — useful for trials, treasures, and grudges.
- **Quickening of the Line.** Inherited · D3 · 4R + 2D + Components (a relative's hair) · Touch · permanent. A newborn of your blood gains a chosen Inherited spell at coming-of-age. Pacts arrive with it.
- **The Long Sleep.** Inherited · D4 · 4R + 3D + Time (1 night) · Self or willing target · until awakened. Enter a death-like slumber that resists time, hunger, and most poisons.

## Tradition: The Hungry

Spells that draw on a fixed external source — a place, a relic, a leyline. Powerful at the source, gutted elsewhere.

- **Drink of the Place.** Hungry · D1 · 0R · Self · 1 scene. While within Long range of a bound source, you regenerate 1 Resolve per round (max your starting value).
- **Borrowed Flame.** Hungry · D2 · 2R · Close · Instant. Hurl a tongue of fire drawn from the source for 3 [CD], Intense, Vicious 1.
- **Shroud of the Hollow Place.** Hungry · D2 · 2R · Self · 1 scene. While within Long range of the source, gain +2 Soak against all damage.
- **Voice of the Old Stone.** Hungry · D2 · 2R · Touch · Instant. Hold an object from the source; hear what was said in its presence in the last day.
- **Hands of the Tide.** Hungry · D3 · 3R + 1D · Long · 1 round. Move a creature or object up to one zone in any direction, even into the air. *Athletics* (D2) resists.
- **Anchor of the Source.** Hungry · D2 · 2R + Components · Touch · 1 day. Bind a minor object as a temporary source-extension; you may cast spells of this tradition through the bound object at +1 difficulty within Close.
- **Calling of the Drowned.** Hungry · D3 · 3R + 2D · Long · 1 round. Summon a single Minion-class servitor of the source's element (a drowned hand, a wind-spirit, a stone-thing). Lasts one round.
- **The Hungry Step.** Hungry · D3 · 3R + 2D · Self · Instant. Teleport up to one Long range, ending in line of sight of the source. May not be used to enter a zone you cannot see.
- **Pact with the Land.** Hungry · D4 · 4R + 3D + Time (1 day) + Components · Self · until broken. Bind yourself to a source as one of its keepers. While at the source, gain +2 to all *Sorcery* tests; while away, suffer –1d20 on the same.
- **Open the Mouth.** Hungry · D5 · 6R + 4D + Time (1 night) + Components (notable offering) · Long · 1 round. The source manifests directly — flood, quake, storm, or signature wonder. Wide-area, gamemaster narrates result. Caster takes 1 Wound and 1 Trauma.

## Designing your own

A useful sanity check for a homebrew spell:

1. **Pick a tradition.** This sets cost shape (Bargained leans on Doom; Hungry leans on locale).
2. **Pick a difficulty.** D1 = parlor; D2 = working sorcery; D3 = serious; D4–D5 = ritual.
3. **Resolve cost = difficulty × 1.** Add Doom if the spell is direct or coercive.
4. **Duration runs from instant through scene to day; longer requires ritual.**
5. **Effect is one sentence.** Damage in [CD], modifiers vs the framework, a clean trigger and a clean outcome.

If you can't write the effect in one sentence, the spell is doing too many things; split it.

## Quick reference

- **Difficulty ladder:** D1–D5 as elsewhere.
- **Resolve cost:** modest at D1, steep at D4+.
- **Failure costs the price anyway.**
- **Petty enchantments** are free or near-free, D0–D1, and the right answer for small magic.
- **Complications** during sorcery are always sorcerous in nature — never just narrative texture.
